Disaster Preparedness Tips for Weather Events

Tornado

  • Shelter-in-Place: Go to a basement or interior, windowless room; cover yourself with sturdy furniture or a mattress.
  • Safe Zones: Avoid windows, exterior walls, and mobile homes. Seek a designated storm shelter if possible.

Flood

  • Evacuation: Follow official orders, know evacuation routes, and avoid floodwaters.
  • Safety: Stay away from water (it may be contaminated or have debris) and avoid downed power lines.
  • Water Use: Boil or use bottled water until declared safe.

Winter Storm

  • Staying Warm: Layer clothing, use blankets, and cover exposed skin.
  • Emergency Kit: Include food, water, flashlight, batteries, first aid, and a shovel.
  • Avoid Frostbite: Watch for numbness or shivering; warm affected areas and seek help if severe.

Heatwave

  • Prevent Heatstroke: Stay in cool areas, avoid peak heat hours, and limit outdoor activity.
  • Hydration: Drink water often; avoid alcohol and caffeine.
  • Cooling Centers: Know nearby cooling center locations for relief.
